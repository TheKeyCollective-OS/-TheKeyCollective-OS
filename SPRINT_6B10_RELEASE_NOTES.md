# Sprint 6B.10 — Agenda and Financial Closure

## Financial Studio
- Added a dedicated full bill checklist beneath the bill manager.
- Every imported and manually entered bill appears in the checklist.
- Each bill has a visible paid checkbox.
- Checked means Paid; unchecked means Still Due.
- Paid date is recorded and persists.
- All, Due, and Paid filters are included.
- Available cash, amount paid, amount still due, cash after unpaid bills, and paid-versus-total count recalculate immediately.

## Agenda

- Colored indicators update immediately after saving.
- Previous and next month arrows are owned by the final Agenda implementation.
- Navigation is available from January 2020 through December 2030, exceeding the December 2027 minimum.
- Next 7 Days and calendar-date taps open the same editable frosted window.
