# Theme Guide

1. Champagne Luxe — warm ivory, champagne gold, espresso ink.
2. Royal Ivory — crisp neutral editorial palette.
3. Midnight Noir — charcoal-blue premium dark theme.
4. Espresso Reserve — walnut and bronze premium dark theme.
5. Obsidian Gold — near-black and restrained gold.
6. Botanical Sage — soft garden green and linen.
7. Rose Quartz — blush and chocolate typography.
8. Ocean Glass — frosted blue-green and navy.
9. Lavender Mist — powder violet and plum.
10. Autumn Manor — tobacco, terracotta, and burnished gold.
11. Sapphire Night — deep jewel blue and warm gold.
12. Desert Bloom — sun-washed clay, ivory, and sage.

All themes define background, surface, primary text, secondary text, accent, border, shadow, hero, and status colors as a coordinated set.
