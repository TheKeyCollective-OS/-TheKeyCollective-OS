# Sprint 6B.12 — Financial Interaction and Theme Refinement

## Financial Studio
- Replaced decorative bill checkboxes with fully interactive touch-friendly checkbox buttons.
- Every imported and manually entered bill appears in one complete list.
- Tapping toggles Still Due and Paid immediately.
- Paid date, paid count, paid total, remaining due, and cash after unpaid bills update immediately.
- Paid state persists through route changes, refresh, closing, and reopening.
- All, Due, and Paid filters remain interactive.

## Sanctuary synchronization
- Sanctuary morning progress is now the shared source used by Sanctuary, Morning Brief, and the Executive Dashboard.
- Counts derive from the actual saved routine length and completion array rather than a hard-coded seven.
- Checking and unchecking morning items updates the saved shared record.
- Morning Brief and Dashboard show the same completed-versus-total value.

## Approved theme refinements
- Coral Society: coral-orange with refined teal accents and pearl surfaces.
- Amber Studio: brighter luxury orange.
- Sunlit Citron: deep golden yellow with dark charcoal typography and no brown cast.
- No other themes or layouts were changed.

## Frozen module
Payments Academy was not modified.
