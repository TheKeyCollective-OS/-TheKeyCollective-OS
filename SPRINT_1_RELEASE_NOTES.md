# Executive Refinement Sprint 1

## What’s new

- Design Studio 2.0 with twelve complete luxury themes.
- New premium dark palettes: Midnight Noir, Espresso Reserve, Obsidian Gold, and Sapphire Night.
- Theme previews and instant theme application.
- Persisted controls for motion, card treatment, corner radius, background texture, and typography.
- My Profile, opened by tapping the founder portrait in the top-right corner.
- Founder Card, Life Score, executive snapshot, editable mission/vision/values, annual theme, five-year vision, legacy statement, quick actions, and achievement cabinet.

## Improved

- Centralized visual tokens for surfaces, text, borders, accents, shadows, and status colors.
- Stronger readable contrast across light and dark palettes.
- Visible keyboard focus states.
- Theme-aware cards, controls, charts, backgrounds, and typography.
- Legacy light/night preferences migrate automatically to Champagne Luxe/Midnight Noir.

## Preserved

- Existing local data and all prior module data.
- Backup, import, recovery, diagnostics, offline support, navigation, and routing.

## Known limitations

- Theme contrast was structurally reviewed and palettes were designed for high readability; final visual acceptance should still be completed on the user’s target iPhone and desktop browsers.
- The profile portrait remains the existing fixed founder image in this sprint.
